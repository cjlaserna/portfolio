My First Sites

My name is Catherine Laserna. I've been teaching myself how to program since I was 8-years-old.

My first horibble website made with HTML, was created when I was about 9 or 10-years-old. It was an attempt to recreate Disney's famous online MMORPG, Pixie Hollow. At the time I was so upset that Disney shut down Toontown and Pixie Hollow, I put my heart into making a 'hate' blog through Google's Blogger and attempting to recreate Pixie Hollow with free web hosting. Although the blog no longer exists, it received well over 3k views in total. The monstrosity that was my "recreation" of Pixie Hollow, received little to no attention. The creation of these sites and the feeling I got making them served as a stepping stone to my CS journey.
